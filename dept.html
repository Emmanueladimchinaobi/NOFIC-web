    DEPARTMENTS/UNITS

    There are five (5) major sections with supportive departments/units in the hospital. The five major sections are based on the five mandates given by the Federal Government to the hospital as Centre of excellence for Treatment, Training, Rehabilitation, Prevention and Research. Major Sections SECTION A – CLINICAL SERVICES Free treatment of genital fistula (obstetric fistula, vesico-vaginal fistula, ureteric fistula, etc) Repair of utero-vaginal prolapse Other general gyneacological surgeries such as myomectomy Renal Dialysis Services In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) Service ENT Services Ophthalmology Services Paediatric/ New Born Care Mental Health Care Urology Services Plastic Surgery General Surgery Internal Medicine Services Family Medicine Services Community Medicine General Outpatient Care Services Maternity Services (Ante-Natal, Delivery and Post Natal Services) Oncology Services (Breast, Cervical, Prostate Cancer Screening and Treatment) Immunization/Vaccine Services Gender Based Violence Services Physiotherapy and Orthopaedic Services Severe Acute Malnutrition Inpatient Care (SAM-IPC) Services Nutrition & Dietetics Radiological Services (Ultrasound, Mammography, X-Ray, etc) Laboratory Services (Molecular, Genetics, Histopathology/Immunochemistry, Haematology/Blood Group Serology and Chemical Pathology, Microbiology) Pharmaceutical Services Family Planning Services Nursing Services Endoscopy SECTION B – TRAINING SERVICES Training of Doctors, Nurses on Fistula Management School of Post-Basic in Oncology Nursing Postgraduate (Certificate, PGD, Masters, PhD in the following areas: Obstetric Fistula, Reproductive Health, Oncology Nursing, Renal Medicine, In-Vitro Fertilization and Embryo Transfer, Surgical Oncology) Residency Training in Obstetric and Gynaecology Internship in Pharmacy, Medical Laboratory Science, Radiology, Nursing and Housemanship SECTION C – PREVENTION SERVICES Community Awareness Campaigns Resource mobilization Mass Screening Capacity Building SECTION D – REHABILITATION SERVICES Vocational Skill Training/Counselling Reintegration of Repaired Clients with their Communities SECTION E – RESEARCH Research in recent advances in Fistula Management Socio-cultural Issues Predisposing factors
